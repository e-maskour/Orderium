# Morocom

## What is Morocom?
Morocom is a **multi-tenant SaaS ERP system** built to streamline business operations — from order management and inventory tracking to invoicing, delivery, and customer interactions — all within a single platform.

## Who is it for?
| Audience | App |
|----------|-----|
| **Business admins** | Backoffice dashboard |
| **Customers** | Client portal |
| **Delivery staff** | Delivery portal |
| **Tenant owners** | Tenant self-service dashboard |

Morocom is designed for **small to medium-sized Moroccan businesses** — wholesalers, distributors, retailers, and service companies — that need a structured, digital way to manage their daily operations without the complexity or cost of enterprise ERP solutions.

## How can it help?
- **Orders & Quotes** — Create, track, and manage orders and quotations end-to-end.
- **Inventory** — Monitor stock levels, adjustments, and warehouse locations in real time.
- **Invoicing & Payments** — Generate invoices, track payments, and manage payment terms.
- **Partners & CRM** — Maintain a unified view of suppliers and customers.
- **Delivery Management** — Assign and track deliveries through a dedicated portal.
- **Multi-tenancy** — Each business operates in an isolated environment on shared infrastructure, keeping data secure and costs low.
- **Notifications** — Real-time push notifications keep all parties informed instantly.

## Built for Moroccan Businesses

Morocco's SME sector faces specific operational challenges: manual paper-based processes, disconnected tools, difficulty tracking cash flow, and limited visibility over stock and deliveries. Morocom addresses these directly:

- **Arabic & French-ready** — Designed with Moroccan business workflows in mind, supporting the multilingual reality of day-to-day operations.
- **Replaces paper & spreadsheets** — Digital orders, quotes, and invoices replace manual bookkeeping, reducing errors and saving time.
- **Cash flow visibility** — Track unpaid invoices, outstanding payments, and payment terms to stay on top of finances.
- **Delivery tracking** — Coordinate last-mile delivery to clients across cities and regions through the dedicated delivery portal.
- **Customer self-service** — Clients can place orders and track their history via the client portal, reducing back-and-forth calls.
- **Affordable SaaS model** — Shared infrastructure keeps costs accessible for businesses that can't invest in on-premise ERP systems.
- **Scalable per tenant** — Whether a business has 2 employees or 200, Morocom scales without requiring infrastructure changes.
